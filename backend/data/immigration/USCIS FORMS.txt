FORM_ID: G-28
FORM_NAME: Notice of Entry of Appearance as Attorney or Accredited Representative
ALIASES: attorney representation form; accredited representative form
CATEGORY: General / Administrative
COMMON_USE: representation before USCIS
KEYWORDS: attorney, lawyer, representative, accredited representative, appearance, legal representation, USCIS
PURPOSE: This form is used when an attorney or accredited representative is officially appearing on behalf of an applicant, petitioner, or respondent in a matter before USCIS.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly submitted when a person wants a lawyer or accredited representative to communicate with USCIS and receive case-related information.
WHO_FILES: Attorney or accredited representative
RELATED_FORMS: G-1145
==================================================

FORM_ID: G-1145
FORM_NAME: E-Notification of Application/Petition Acceptance
ALIASES: e-notification form; text message receipt form; email notification form
CATEGORY: General / Administrative
COMMON_USE: case receipt notifications
KEYWORDS: e-notification, text alert, email alert, receipt notice, acceptance, USCIS
PURPOSE: This form is used to request electronic notification by text message and/or email when USCIS accepts an application or petition at a lockbox facility.
WHEN_USED: It is usually clipped to the front of a filing package so the applicant can quickly receive confirmation that USCIS accepted the filing.
WHO_FILES: Applicant or petitioner
RELATED_FORMS: I-130, I-485, I-765, N-400
==================================================

FORM_ID: G-884
FORM_NAME: Request for Return of Original Documents
ALIASES: return original documents form
CATEGORY: General / Administrative
COMMON_USE: requesting original records back
KEYWORDS: return documents, original documents, evidence, submitted records, USCIS
PURPOSE: This form is used to ask USCIS to return original documents that were previously submitted.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly used after a case is completed or when a person needs important original documents back.
WHO_FILES: Applicant, petitioner, or authorized representative
RELATED_FORMS: G-28
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-130
FORM_NAME: Petition for Alien Relative
ALIASES: family petition; relative petition
CATEGORY: Family-Based Immigration
COMMON_USE: sponsoring a qualifying family member
KEYWORDS: spouse petition, parent petition, child petition, family green card, relative, petitioner
PURPOSE: This form is used to establish a qualifying family relationship between a U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident and a relative who wants to immigrate.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly the first step in family-based immigration cases involving spouses, parents, children, and siblings where eligible.
WHO_FILES: U.S. citizen or lawful permanent resident petitioner
RELATED_FORMS: I-485, I-864, I-130A
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-129F
FORM_NAME: Petition for Alien Fiancé(e)
ALIASES: fiance visa form; K-1 petition
CATEGORY: Family-Based Immigration
COMMON_USE: bringing a fiancé(e) to the United States
KEYWORDS: fiance, fiancée, K-1 visa, marriage petition, engagement, USCIS
PURPOSE: This form is used by a U.S. citizen to request permission for a foreign fiancé(e) to come to the United States for marriage.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when the couple plans to marry in the United States within the required period after entry.
WHO_FILES: U.S. citizen petitioner
RELATED_FORMS: I-485
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-485
FORM_NAME: Application to Register Permanent Residence or Adjust Status
ALIASES: green card application; adjustment of status form
CATEGORY: Green Card / Permanent Residence
COMMON_USE: applying for a green card from inside the United States
KEYWORDS: adjustment of status, green card, permanent residence, lawful permanent resident, AOS
PURPOSE: This form is the main application used by eligible individuals in the United States to apply for lawful permanent resident status, also known as a green card.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly filed after an immigrant petition is approved or when a visa number is available and the applicant is eligible to adjust status.
WHO_FILES: Eligible applicant inside the United States
RELATED_FORMS: I-130, I-140, I-765, I-131, I-864, I-693
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-751
FORM_NAME: Petition to Remove Conditions on Residence
ALIASES: remove conditions form; conditional green card removal
CATEGORY: Green Card / Permanent Residence
COMMON_USE: converting a 2-year conditional green card into a 10-year green card
KEYWORDS: remove conditions, marriage green card, conditional resident, joint filing, waiver
PURPOSE: This form is used to remove the conditions on permanent residence for someone who received a 2-year conditional green card, often through marriage.
WHEN_USED: It is generally filed during the 90-day period before the conditional resident card expires, unless a waiver basis applies.
WHO_FILES: Conditional permanent resident, often jointly with spouse
RELATED_FORMS: I-90
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-90
FORM_NAME: Application to Replace Permanent Resident Card
ALIASES: green card renewal form; replace green card form
CATEGORY: Green Card / Permanent Residence
COMMON_USE: renewing or replacing a green card
KEYWORDS: green card renewal, replacement card, lost green card, expired card, permanent resident card
PURPOSE: This form is used to renew or replace a Permanent Resident Card, also known as a green card.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when a green card is expiring, lost, stolen, damaged, or contains incorrect information.
WHO_FILES: Lawful permanent resident
RELATED_FORMS: I-751, N-400
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-129
FORM_NAME: Petition for a Nonimmigrant Worker
ALIASES: work visa petition; temporary worker petition
CATEGORY: Employment-Based Immigration
COMMON_USE: requesting temporary worker classification
KEYWORDS: H-1B, L-1, O-1, P visa, R-1, TN-related employer filing, nonimmigrant worker
PURPOSE: This form is used by employers to petition for a foreign national to work in the United States in certain temporary or nonimmigrant classifications.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly filed for work visa categories such as H-1B, L-1, O-1, P, and R classifications.
WHO_FILES: U.S. employer or qualifying petitioner
RELATED_FORMS: I-539, I-765
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-140
FORM_NAME: Immigrant Petition for Alien Worker
ALIASES: employment green card petition; immigrant worker petition
CATEGORY: Employment-Based Immigration
COMMON_USE: sponsoring a worker for permanent residence
KEYWORDS: employment-based green card, EB-1, EB-2, EB-3, immigrant worker, PERM
PURPOSE: This form is used by an employer, and in some categories by the individual, to petition for an immigrant worker to become a lawful permanent resident.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly filed in employment-based immigrant visa categories as part of the green card process.
WHO_FILES: Employer or self-petitioning individual where allowed
RELATED_FORMS: I-485
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-765
FORM_NAME: Application for Employment Authorization
ALIASES: work permit form; EAD application
CATEGORY: Employment / Benefits
COMMON_USE: applying for a work permit
KEYWORDS: work permit, EAD, employment authorization document, authorization to work
PURPOSE: This form is used by eligible noncitizens to request employment authorization and receive an Employment Authorization Document.
WHEN_USED: It is often filed with or after another immigration application, such as adjustment of status, asylum, TPS, or certain student-related categories.
WHO_FILES: Eligible applicant
RELATED_FORMS: I-485, I-589, I-821
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-526E
FORM_NAME: Immigrant Petition by Regional Center Investor
ALIASES: EB-5 investor petition; regional center investor form
CATEGORY: Employment-Based Immigration / Investor
COMMON_USE: seeking permanent residence through qualifying investment
KEYWORDS: EB-5, investor visa, regional center, investment immigration, job creation
PURPOSE: This form is used by certain immigrant investors associated with a regional center to request classification under the EB-5 immigrant investor program.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when an investor seeks a green card based on a qualifying investment that meets EB-5 program requirements.
WHO_FILES: Qualifying immigrant investor
RELATED_FORMS: I-485, I-829
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-539
FORM_NAME: Application To Extend/Change Nonimmigrant Status
ALIASES: extend stay form; change status form
CATEGORY: Nonimmigrant Status
COMMON_USE: extending or changing temporary immigration status
KEYWORDS: extend visa status, change status, visitor extension, student status, dependent status
PURPOSE: This form is used by certain nonimmigrants to request an extension of stay or a change from one nonimmigrant status to another.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly filed by visitors, students, dependents, and others who are eligible to extend or change status without leaving the United States.
WHO_FILES: Eligible nonimmigrant applicant
RELATED_FORMS: I-129, I-94
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-94
FORM_NAME: Arrival/Departure Record
ALIASES: I-94 travel record; entry record
CATEGORY: Nonimmigrant Status / Travel Record
COMMON_USE: proving lawful entry and authorized stay
KEYWORDS: arrival record, departure record, lawful entry, admission record, class of admission
PURPOSE: This record documents a noncitizen's admission into the United States, including class of admission and authorized period of stay.
WHEN_USED: It is used to verify status, entry date, and expiration or duration of authorized stay.
WHO_FILES: Not typically filed as a standard USCIS application; record issued by government systems
RELATED_FORMS: I-102, I-539
==================================================

FORM_ID: N-400
FORM_NAME: Application for Naturalization
ALIASES: citizenship application; naturalization form
CATEGORY: Citizenship / Naturalization
COMMON_USE: applying to become a U.S. citizen
KEYWORDS: naturalization, citizenship, civics test, English test, oath ceremony
PURPOSE: This form is used by lawful permanent residents and certain other eligible individuals to apply for U.S. citizenship through naturalization.
WHEN_USED: It is filed after meeting eligibility requirements such as residency, physical presence, and good moral character.
WHO_FILES: Eligible applicant for naturalization
RELATED_FORMS: N-648, N-336, I-90
==================================================

FORM_ID: N-600
FORM_NAME: Application for Certificate of Citizenship
ALIASES: certificate of citizenship form
CATEGORY: Citizenship / Naturalization
COMMON_USE: obtaining formal proof of citizenship
KEYWORDS: citizenship proof, derived citizenship, acquired citizenship, certificate
PURPOSE: This form is used to obtain a Certificate of Citizenship for a person who already became a U.S. citizen through parents by birth abroad or derivation.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when someone needs formal evidence of citizenship but is not applying to naturalize.
WHO_FILES: U.S. citizen applicant or parent/legal guardian on behalf of child
RELATED_FORMS: N-400
==================================================

CATEGORY: Citizenship / Naturalization
COMMON_USE: challenging a denied naturalization application
KEYWORDS: N-400 denial, hearing request, naturalization review, citizenship appeal
PURPOSE: This form is used to request a hearing after USCIS denies a naturalization application.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when an applicant believes USCIS wrongly denied the N-400 and wants administrative review.

RELATED_FORMS: N-400
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-589
FORM_NAME: Application for Asylum and for Withholding of Removal
ALIASES: asylum form; withholding of removal application
CATEGORY: Humanitarian
COMMON_USE: seeking protection from persecution
KEYWORDS: asylum, persecution, refugee protection, fear of return, withholding of removal
PURPOSE: This form is used by individuals in the United States to request asylum and withholding of removal based on fear of persecution in their home country.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when a person seeks humanitarian protection because they fear harm based on a protected ground.
WHO_FILES: Applicant seeking asylum or withholding
RELATED_FORMS: I-765
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-918
FORM_NAME: Petition for U Nonimmigrant Status
ALIASES: U visa form; crime victim visa petition
CATEGORY: Humanitarian
COMMON_USE: immigration relief for victims who assist law enforcement
KEYWORDS: U visa, crime victim, law enforcement certification, victim protection, humanitarian relief
PURPOSE: This form is used by eligible victims of certain crimes who have suffered abuse and have been helpful, are being helpful, or are likely to be helpful to law enforcement.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when a qualifying crime victim seeks temporary lawful status and related immigration protection.
WHO_FILES: Eligible crime victim or qualifying applicant
RELATED_FORMS: I-192, I-765
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-360
FORM_NAME: Petition for Amerasian, Widow(er), or Special Immigrant
ALIASES: VAWA self-petition form; special immigrant petition
CATEGORY: Humanitarian / Special Immigrant
COMMON_USE: special immigrant and certain humanitarian classifications
KEYWORDS: VAWA, widow petition, religious worker, special immigrant juvenile, SIJ
PURPOSE: This form is used for several special immigrant categories, including certain abused spouses, children, and parents under VAWA, widows or widowers of U.S. citizens, religious workers, and some other special classifications.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when a person qualifies under one of the special immigrant categories recognized by immigration law.
WHO_FILES: Eligible self-petitioner or applicant in a qualifying category
RELATED_FORMS: I-485
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-131
FORM_NAME: Application for Travel Document
ALIASES: advance parole form; reentry permit form; refugee travel document form
CATEGORY: Travel / Re-Entry
COMMON_USE: requesting permission or documentation for travel
KEYWORDS: advance parole, reentry permit, refugee travel document, travel authorization
PURPOSE: This form is used to request certain travel documents, including advance parole, refugee travel documents, and re-entry permits.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly filed by adjustment applicants, permanent residents who need a re-entry permit, and certain humanitarian applicants.
WHO_FILES: Eligible applicant
RELATED_FORMS: I-485
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-102
FORM_NAME: Application for Replacement/Initial Nonimmigrant Arrival-Departure Document
ALIASES: replace I-94 form
CATEGORY: Travel / Records
COMMON_USE: replacing or obtaining an I-94 document
KEYWORDS: replace I-94, initial I-94, arrival departure document, correction of record
PURPOSE: This form is used to request a replacement or, in certain situations, an initial Arrival-Departure Document.
WHEN_USED: It is filed when a nonimmigrant needs a replacement I-94 or a proper arrival-departure record.
WHO_FILES: Eligible nonimmigrant applicant

==================================================

FORM_ID: I-290B
FORM_NAME: Notice of Appeal or Motion
ALIASES: USCIS appeal form; motion to reopen form; motion to reconsider form
CATEGORY: Appeals / Motions
COMMON_USE: appealing or challenging a USCIS decision
KEYWORDS: appeal, motion to reopen, motion to reconsider, adverse decision, USCIS denial
PURPOSE: This form is used to file an appeal with the Administrative Appeals Office in eligible cases or to ask USCIS to reopen or reconsider a decision.
WHEN_USED: It is filed after a denial, revocation, or other unfavorable decision when the form instructions permit appeal or motion practice.
WHO_FILES: Applicant, petitioner, or authorized representative
RELATED_FORMS: G-28
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-864
FORM_NAME: Affidavit of Support Under Section 213A of the INA
ALIASES: affidavit of support form; sponsor financial support form
CATEGORY: Financial Support
COMMON_USE: proving a sponsor can financially support an immigrant
KEYWORDS: sponsor, income requirement, financial support, family immigration, public charge
PURPOSE: This form is used to show that an intending immigrant has adequate financial support and is not likely to become primarily dependent on the government.
WHEN_USED: It is commonly required in family-based green card cases and some employment-based cases when a relative has an ownership interest.
WHO_FILES: Sponsoring relative or joint sponsor
RELATED_FORMS: I-130, I-485
==================================================

FORM_ID: I-912
FORM_NAME: Request for Fee Waiver
ALIASES: USCIS fee waiver form
CATEGORY: Financial Support / Fees
COMMON_USE: asking USCIS to waive filing fees
KEYWORDS: fee waiver, low income, inability to pay, filing fee, USCIS fees
PURPOSE: This form is used to request that USCIS waive certain filing fees for eligible applicants who cannot afford to pay.
WHEN_USED: It is filed with eligible applications or petitions when the person qualifies based on means-tested benefits, low income, or financial hardship.
WHO_FILES: Eligible applicant or petitioner
RELATED_FORMS: N-400, I-90, I-765
==================================================
