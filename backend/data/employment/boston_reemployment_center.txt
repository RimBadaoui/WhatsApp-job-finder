Boston Re-Employment Center (REC)

Source:
https://www.mass.gov/info-details/the-boston-re-employment-center-rec

----------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
The Boston Re-Employment Center (REC) provides in-person and appointment-based services to help individuals with unemployment insurance and job search assistance.

It is operated by the Massachusetts Department of Unemployment Assistance (DUA) and supports individuals who are unemployed or receiving unemployment benefits.

Services are available by appointment only.

----------------------------------------
KEY USER ACTIONS (IMPORTANT)
----------------------------------------

Schedule an Appointment (REQUIRED):
https://www.mass.gov/info-details/make-an-appointment-with-dua

- Must book an appointment before visiting
- Available Monday – Friday
- Services typically offered 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM

----------------------------------------
CORE SERVICES
----------------------------------------

Unemployment Insurance Help:
- Assistance filing unemployment claims
- Help resolving claim issues or delays
- Support with identity verification and documentation
- Guidance on eligibility and benefits

Job Search Assistance:
- Resume support
- Job search guidance
- Referrals to employment programs and services

Reemployment Support:
- Helps individuals return to work faster
- May connect users to career services and training programs

----------------------------------------
PROGRAM CONNECTIONS
----------------------------------------

RESEA Program (Reemployment Services and Eligibility Assessment):
https://www.mass.gov/info-details/reemployment-services-and-eligibility-assessment-resea

- Required for some unemployment recipients
- Provides structured job search support
- Helps ensure continued eligibility for benefits

WHO THIS HELPS
----------------------------------------

- Individuals receiving unemployment benefits
- Recently unemployed workers

----------------------------------------
WHAT TO EXPECT
----------------------------------------

- Appointment-based services (no walk-ins)
- Staff assistance with both unemployment and job search
- Possible referrals to career centers (MassHire, Goodwill, etc.)
- Structured support if enrolled in RESEA program
