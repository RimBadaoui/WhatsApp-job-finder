Goodwill Boston Career Center

Source:
https://careercenter.goodwillmass.org/

----------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
Goodwill Boston Career Center provides free career services to help individuals:
- Find jobs
- Build resumes
- Prepare for interviews
- Connect with employers

----------------------------------------
CORE SERVICES
----------------------------------------
Career Support & Coaching:
- One-on-one job coaching
- Individual employment plans
- Career counseling
- Mock interviews
- LinkedIn and job search strategy training

Job Search Assistance:
- Access to job postings (including exclusive listings)
- Help applying to jobs online
- Resume and cover letter support
- Applicant tracking system (ATS) strategies

Workshops & Training:
- Resume writing workshops
- Interview preparation sessions
- Online job search training
- Career orientation programs

----------------------------------------
TRAINING PROGRAMS
----------------------------------------
Career Readiness:
- First Step Program
- HELP (Human Services Employment Ladder Program)

Workforce Training:
- Industry-specific training (human services, retail, admin)
- Soft skills development (communication, teamwork, time management)

On-the-Job Training:
- Work experience through Goodwill programs
- Placement support

----------------------------------------
EMPLOYER CONNECTIONS
----------------------------------------
- Direct connections with hiring employers
- On-site recruitment events
- Pre-screened job matching

Hot Jobs Page:
https://careercenter.goodwillmass.org/hot-jobs/

----------------------------------------
SPECIAL SUPPORT SERVICES
----------------------------------------
- Support for individuals with disabilities
- Case management for barriers to employment
- Career change support

Veterans Services:
- Dedicated veteran support

Clothing Support:
- Free professional clothing for job seekers

----------------------------------------
ADDITIONAL RESOURCES
----------------------------------------
- Resume tips and job search guides
- Digital and AI skills training
- Online career development tools

----------------------------------------
HOW TO GET STARTED
----------------------------------------
1. Visit: https://careercenter.goodwillmass.org/
2. Attend Career Center Orientation
3. Meet with a career navigator
4. Build resume and job search plan
5. Attend workshops or training
6. Apply to jobs and attend recruitment events
