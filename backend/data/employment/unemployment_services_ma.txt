Massachusetts Department of Unemployment Assistance (DUA)

Source:
https://www.mass.gov/orgs/department-of-unemployment-assistance

----------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
The Massachusetts Department of Unemployment Assistance (DUA) provides temporary income (unemployment insurance) to eligible workers who have lost their jobs or had their hours reduced.

The goal is to support individuals financially while they actively search for new employment.

----------------------------------------
KEY USER ACTIONS
----------------------------------------

Apply for Unemployment Benefits:
https://www.mass.gov/how-to/apply-for-unemployment-insurance-benefits
- Create a MyMassGov account
- Submit an unemployment claim online
- Provide employment history and identity verification

Login to Unemployment Account:
https://www.mass.gov/how-to/log-in-to-unemployment-services-for-workers

File Weekly Claim (REQUIRED to get paid):
https://www.mass.gov/how-to/file-your-weekly-unemployment-claim
- Must be done every week
- Report job search activity
- Report any income earned

----------------------------------------
ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------
- Must have lost job or had hours reduced through no fault of your own
- Must be able to work
- Must be available for work
- Must be actively searching for a job
- Must meet wage and work history requirements

----------------------------------------
BENEFITS INFORMATION
----------------------------------------
- Weekly financial payments based on prior earnings
- Duration depends on eligibility and state rules
- Direct deposit or debit card payment options
