Boston Public Library (BPL) - Job & Career Success

Source:
https://www.bpl.org/job-career-success/

----------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
The Boston Public Library (BPL) offers free job and career services to help individuals find employment, improve skills, and prepare for career advancement.

Services include:
- One-on-one job assistance
- Resume and cover letter help
- Interview preparation
- Career development events
- Online learning resources

The BPL acts as a one-stop resource for job seekers and provides both in-person and online support.
