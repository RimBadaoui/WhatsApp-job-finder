Training Opportunities Program (TOP)

Source:
https://www.mass.gov/info-details/training-opportunities-program-top

----------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
The Training Opportunities Program (TOP) allows individuals receiving unemployment benefits in Massachusetts to attend approved full-time training programs while continuing to receive those benefits.

The goal of TOP is to help job seekers gain new skills and improve their chances of finding employment in high-demand fields.

----------------------------------------
KEY BENEFIT (IMPORTANT)
----------------------------------------

Continue Unemployment Benefits While Training:
- Participants can collect unemployment insurance (UI) while enrolled in approved full-time training programs
- Removes the requirement to actively search for work while in training (if approved)

----------------------------------------
KEY USER ACTIONS
----------------------------------------

Learn About Program:
https://www.mass.gov/info-details/training-opportunities-program-top

Apply for TOP:
https://www.mass.gov/how-to/apply-for-the-training-opportunities-program-top

- Must apply after enrolling in an approved training program
- Must be receiving unemployment benefits
- Application must be approved by DUA

----------------------------------------
ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------

To qualify for TOP:
- Must be receiving unemployment insurance (UI)
- Must be enrolled in a full-time training program
- Training must be approved by the Department of Unemployment Assistance (DUA)
- Training must improve employment opportunities

----------------------------------------
TRAINING REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------

Training must be:
- Full-time (typically 20+ hours per week of instruction)
- From an approved training provider
- Focused on improving job prospects

Examples:
- Certificate programs
- Degree programs
- Vocational or technical training

----------------------------------------
PROGRAM BENEFITS
----------------------------------------

- Continue receiving unemployment payments during training
- Gain new skills for better job opportunities
- Access to approved training programs in high-demand industries

----------------------------------------
HOW TO APPLY (STEP-BY-STEP)
----------------------------------------

1. Choose a training program
   - Must meet full-time requirements
   - Must be approved or approvable by DUA

2. Enroll in the training program

3. Apply for TOP:
   https://www.mass.gov/how-to/apply-for-the-training-opportunities-program-top

4. Submit required documentation
   - Training details
   - Enrollment verification

5. Wait for approval from DUA

----------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES
----------------------------------------

- Apply as early as possible after enrolling in training
- Not all training programs are automatically approved
- Approval is required before benefits can continue under TOP

----------------------------------------
RELATED PROGRAMS
----------------------------------------

MassHire Career Centers:
- Help find approved training programs
- Provide guidance on eligibility and application

RESEA Program:
- May connect individuals to training opportunities

----------------------------------------
WHO THIS HELPS
----------------------------------------

- Unemployed individuals looking to gain new skills
- Career changers
- Workers whose industries are declining
- Individuals needing certification or training to re-enter the workforce
