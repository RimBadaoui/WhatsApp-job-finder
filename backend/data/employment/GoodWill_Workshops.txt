Page 2 - Workshop Descriptions

Ace the Interview
Learn actionable strategies that will give you the confidence to ace your next job interview.

Career Center Orientation
In this workshop, you will learn about all of the services and resources that Goodwill Boston Career Center provides. Additionally, you will review the basics of preparing for your job search, and walk away with a Career Action Plan.

DTA Assistance - Office Hours
Meet with a DTA representative to discuss resources and opportunities that may be available to you.

Intro to Basic Labor Market Information
This workshop will help provide you with valuable tools to learn about high demand career pathways in the Boston area.

Open Assistance Lab
In this lab, you will receive career navigation and guidance on updating your resume, applying to a job, developing a resume, researching Labor Market Information, etc.

Resume Beginnings (Part I)
This workshop will help you understand what employers look for in a resume. It covers the layout, content, and how the words you use can increase your chances of getting an interview. This workshop is intended for job seekers who do not have a resume or their resume is outdated.

Refining Your Resume (Part II)
This workshop is intended for job seekers who are seeking to refine their existing resume or those who have taken Winning Resumes Part I. In this workshop, you will be able to meet 1:1 with a Career Navigator to refine your resume. Formatting, AI tools and resources, and cover letters will be reviewed.

Online Job Searching
In this workshop you will learn the basics of using online job search platforms and techniques to stand out in applicant tracking systems.

Hot Jobs with the Business Service Team
In this workshop, a member of the Goodwill Boston Career Center Business Service Team will review in-demand industries and Boston area employers who are actively recruiting for entry and mid-level positions. Companies holding on-site recruitment events will be reviewed. Bring your resume so that staff can assist you in identifying jobs that would be a good fit for you.
