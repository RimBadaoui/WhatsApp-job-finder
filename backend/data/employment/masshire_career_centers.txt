MassHire Downtown Boston Career Center
Source:
https://masshiredowntownboston.org/

----------------------------------------
OVERVIEW
----------------------------------------
MassHire Downtown Boston Career Center provides FREE career services to job seekers, including coaching, job search support, training programs, and connections to employers.

It is part of the statewide MassHire workforce system, designed to connect job seekers with employment opportunities and help businesses find qualified workers.

----------------------------------------

- Vocational training opportunities (e.g., carpentry, HVAC)
- Skills development programs

Training Grants:

- Training Opportunities Program (TOP)

RESEA Program:
- Reemployment Services and Eligibility Assessment
- Helps unemployment recipients return to work faster
