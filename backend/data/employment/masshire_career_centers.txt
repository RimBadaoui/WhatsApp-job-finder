Program: MassHire Career Centers

MassHire Career Centers provide free employment services to job seekers in Massachusetts.

Services include:
- job search assistance
- resume and cover letter help
- interview preparation
- career counseling
- job training opportunities
- job fairs and hiring events

Locations:
MassHire Career Centers operate throughout Massachusetts including Boston.

How to get help:
Job seekers can visit a MassHire Career Center in person or access services online.
